FAQ

Do I need an account?

No. You can run previews immediately. Creating a free account unlocks history and higher limits.

Which payments do you support?

International cards via Stripe for Starter, Pro, and Advanced ($9.99 / $19.99 / $39.99 per month). Optional legacy IRR cart remains in the dashboard where applicable.

Can I translate subtitles?

Yes. Paid plans can translate SRT, transcript, and summary text into multiple languages.
