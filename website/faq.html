Creator Styles

Bold, high-contrast captions with emphasis on key words — designed for talking-head hooks and direct-response clips. Yellow highlights on punchlines, tight timing on fast speech.

Large, energetic typography with strong color pops — built for retention on fast-paced challenge and reaction content. Words scale up on emphasis beats.

Clean serif-inspired captions with generous spacing and muted colors — suited for premium brands, real estate, finance and lifestyle content where readability matters more than flash.

Lower-third friendly captions with readable line lengths — ideal for interview clips, podcast highlights and talking-head excerpts where the speaker should stay visible.

Pick from curated creator presets and preview before export. Fine-tune caption text and timing in the editor. Full custom font/color controls are on the roadmap — Pro and Business plans get the full preset library today.
