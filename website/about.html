About Cutup

Cutup is a platform for downloading, transcribing, and summarizing video and audio. We combine modern AI with a simple workflow so you can go from a link or file to subtitles, full text, and key takeaways in record time—without drowning in manual edits.

Our mission

We want rich media to be easy to reuse and understand. Whether you need captions for social clips, a transcript for notes, or a short summary for your team, Cutup is built to save time without sacrificing quality.

What you can do

  • ✓ Download media: Audio and video from supported platforms, with quality options where available.
  • ✓ Speech to text: High-accuracy transcription for long-form content.
  • ✓ AI summaries: Concise summaries and key points from transcripts.
  • ✓ Subtitles & translation: SRT export and translation on paid plans.
  • ✓ Uploads: Process your own audio and video files.

Technology

Cutup uses industry-standard models for transcription and summarization so results stay accurate and useful for real workflows—not just demos.
