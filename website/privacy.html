Privacy policy

At Cutup we respect your privacy and work to protect personal information you share with us.

What we collect

We only collect data needed to run the service:

  • • Account details when you sign in (for example via Google).
  • • Usage and subscription metadata required for limits and billing.
  • • Content you submit (URLs, uploads) solely to process your request.

How we use information

We use your information to:

  • • Provide and improve Cutup features.
  • • Manage your account and plan.
  • • Process files and links you send for transcription, summary, or download.

Security

We use HTTPS for transport security. Uploaded and generated content should be handled according to our retention practices on the server; contact us if you need a data-processing summary for your organization.

Cookies

We may use cookies or similar storage to keep you signed in and to remember preferences. You can control cookies in your browser settings.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated on the site or by email where appropriate.

Contact

For privacy questions, email support@cutup.shop.
